About Me

With over 25 years of classroom and one-on-one teaching experience, I bring deep expertise, patience, and genuine enthusiasm to every session. My goal is simple: help each student build confidence, find their learning style, and reach their full potential.

I work with students from kindergarten through 8th grade in reading, math, writing, and general academics. Sessions take place at my home classroom in Sleepy Hollow with flexible scheduling to fit your family.

Having spent years teaching and tutoring in Catholic school settings, I have a deep familiarity with the curriculum, values, and expectations of Catholic education in the Northwest Suburbs. I understand what these schools expect and how to help students thrive in them.

I am trained in the UFLI Foundations reading program and hold a Master's degree in Teacher Leadership. Illinois-certified through grade 9 with a special endorsement in Reading.

  • M.A. Teacher Leadership — North Park University (2001)
  • B.A. Elementary Education — Purdue University (1995)
  • Illinois Certified: K–9 General Education
  • Illinois Certified: Reading, Upper Elementary / Junior High
  • Trained in UFLI Foundations Reading Program
  • Experience in Catholic school settings, K–8
  • 20+ years in Illinois public & private schools
  • Private tutor, Grades K–8, since May 2019
